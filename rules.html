Student Conduct Guide

Important guidelines for new students

Code of Conduct

All students are expected to conduct themselves in a manner that upholds the reputation and values of the Nigerian Army University, Biu.

General Conduct

  • Maintain discipline and respect for self and others
  • Be punctual to classes and all university activities
  • Dress appropriately and maintain proper grooming
  • Use appropriate language at all times
  • Respect university property and facilities

Academic Integrity

  • Do not engage in academic dishonesty (cheating, plagiarism)
  • Attend all scheduled classes
  • Complete assignments and examinations honestly
  • Respect intellectual property rights
  • Follow all examination rules

Prohibited Activities

  • Ragging or harassment of any kind
  • Possession of weapons or dangerous items
  • Use of alcohol or illegal substances on campus
  • Violence or physical assault
  • Theft or vandalism
  • Unauthorized political activities

Hostel Rules

  • Keep your room clean and tidy
  • No unauthorized guests in hostels
  • Observe quiet hours
  • Follow all fire safety regulations
  • Report any maintenance issues promptly

Disciplinary Actions

Violation of any of these rules may result in:

  • Warning
  • Suspension
  • Expulsion
  • Other disciplinary measures as deemed appropriate

Reporting Issues

If you witness any misconduct or need to report an issue, contact:

  • Student Affairs: +234 800 NAUB STD
  • Security: +234 800 NAUB SEC
