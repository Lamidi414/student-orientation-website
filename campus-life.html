Campus Life

Discover life at NAUB

Campus Facilities

NAUB provides excellent facilities to support your academic and personal development.

Library

The university library offers:

  • Extensive collection of textbooks and reference materials
  • Digital resources and online databases
  • Study rooms and quiet zones
  • Computer workstations
  • Opening Hours: Monday - Friday 8AM - 10PM, Saturday 9AM - 6PM

Sports Facilities

  • Football field
  • Basketball court
  • Volleyball court
  • Tennis court
  • Gymnasium
  • Sports complex

ICT Center

  • Computer laboratories with internet access
  • Wi-Fi connectivity across campus
  • IT training and support services

Health Center

The university health center provides:

  • 24/7 medical services
  • First aid and emergency care
  • Health counseling
  • Medical referrals

Student Activities

Get involved in campus life through various clubs, societies, and activities.

Clubs & Societies

  • Academic clubs
  • Sports clubs
  • Cultural organizations
  • Volunteer groups
  • Religious societies

Student Union

The Students' Union represents student interests and organizes various activities throughout the academic year.

Events

  • Orientation week
  • Sports competitions
  • Cultural festivals
  • Academic conferences
  • Career fairs

Hostel Accommodation

On-campus housing is available for students who need it.

Hostel Features

  • Single and shared rooms
  • 24-hour security
  • Common areas and study rooms
  • Laundry facilities
  • Internet connectivity

How to Apply

Apply through the student portal. Accommodation is allocated on a first-come, first-served basis with priority given to fresh students.
