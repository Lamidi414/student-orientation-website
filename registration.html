Registration Guide

Complete your NAUB enrollment

Step-by-Step Registration Process

Step 1: Log In to Student Portal

Visit the student portal and log in using your matric number and password. If you haven't set up your password, visit the IT Help Desk.

Step 2: Complete Bio-data Verification

Verify and update your personal information including name, date of birth, contact details, and guardian/parent information.

Step 3: Course Registration

Select your courses for the semester. Ensure you meet all prerequisites and don't exceed the maximum credit load.

Step 4: Payment of Fees

Proceed to pay your tuition and other fees through the designated bank or online payment portal. Keep all payment receipts.

Step 5: Generate Registration Slip

After successful payment, generate your official registration slip from the portal.

Step 6: Departmental Clearance

Visit your department for clearance. Bring your registration slip and payment receipts.

Step 7: Obtain Student ID Card

Process your student ID card at the Registry Office with your registration slip and passport photographs.

Important Deadlines

  • Course Registration: Within first two weeks of semester
  • Fee Payment: Before course registration
  • Departmental Clearance: Within first month

Important Notes

  • Late registration attracts a penalty fee
  • All courses must be approved by your academic advisor
  • Keep copies of all receipts and documents
  • Report any discrepancies immediately
