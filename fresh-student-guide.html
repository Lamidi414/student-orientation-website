Fresh Student Guide

Your complete guide to starting at NAUB

Welcome to NAUB

Congratulations on your admission to the Nigerian Army University, Biu! This guide will help you navigate your transition into university life.

Before You Arrive

  • Complete your course registration on the student portal
  • Pay your tuition and accommodation fees
  • Apply for hostel accommodation (if needed)
  • Activate your official NAUB email account
  • Medical examination and certificate

What to Bring

  • Original admission letter
  • Valid ID (JAMB admission slip, etc.)
  • Passport photographs (multiple copies)
  • Medical certificate
  • Payment receipts
  • Basic toiletries and clothing
  • Study materials

First Week Checklist

  • Attend orientation program
  • Obtain your student ID card
  • Visit your department for clearance
  • Collect your course materials
  • Register for library card
  • Join class WhatsApp groups
  • Explore campus facilities

Important Contacts

  • Student Affairs: +234 800 NAUB STD
  • IT Support: +234 800 NAUB IT
  • Health Center: +234 800 NAUB HLT
  • Security: +234 800 NAUB SEC

Tips for Success

  • Attend all your classes
  • Manage your time effectively
  • Form study groups with classmates
  • Use the library regularly
  • Participate in student activities
  • Seek help when needed
