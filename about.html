About NAUB

Learn about the Nigerian Army University, Biu

Welcome to NAUB

The Nigerian Army University, Biu (NAUB) is a premier military-backed institution of higher learning established to provide quality education in various fields of study. The university is committed to producing graduates who are not only academically excellent but also disciplined and morally upright.

Our Mission

To produce well-rounded graduates equipped with theoretical knowledge and practical skills, characterized by discipline, leadership, and innovation to meet national and global challenges.

Our Vision

To become a world-class university recognized for academic excellence, research innovation, and producing graduates who contribute meaningfully to national development.

Core Values

  • Discipline - Upholding the highest standards of conduct and order
  • Excellence - Striving for the highest quality in all endeavors
  • Integrity - Maintaining honesty and moral principles
  • Innovation - Embracing creativity and new ideas
  • Service - Commitment to national development

Faculties & Departments

NAUB offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple faculties:

  • Faculty of Engineering
  • Faculty of Sciences
  • Faculty of Arts
  • Faculty of Social Sciences
  • Faculty of Management Sciences
  • Faculty of Education

Campus Life

NAUB provides a vibrant campus life with various student activities, clubs, societies, and sports facilities. Students have access to modern lecture halls, well-equipped libraries, laboratories, and recreational centers.
